What is Capture Match and what does it do?

Capture Match is your one-stop platform for connecting with talented photographers and capturing stunning photos for any occasion. We simplify the process for both clients seeking photography services and photographers looking to showcase their work and grow their business.

Is there a fee for using Capture Match?

Absolutely not! Signing up and creating a profile on Capture Match is completely free for both clients and photographers. You can browse photographer portfolios, search for specific services, and connect with potential clients without any upfront cost.

How do I sign up to be a photographer on Capture Match?

Fill out a short registration form with your basic information and contact details. Upload high-quality photos that represent your unique style and expertise. The more visually compelling your portfolio, the more likely you are to attract potential clients.

How do I book a photography session with a photographer?

Once you've found the ideal photographer, click the "Contact" button on their profile. This opens a secure messaging system within Capture Match. Use the messaging system to discuss your specific needs, preferred date and time, and any questions you might have.
